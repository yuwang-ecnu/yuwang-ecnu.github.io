About [CV]

I am currently a visting scholar at University of Utah, co-hosted by Prof. Zhou Yu and Prof. Yehua (Dennis) Wei.

I am also a final-year Ph.D. student at East China Normal University, Department of Regional Economics, advised by Prof. Bindong Sun

I mainly work on research topics including City Size Distribution, Urban Hierarchy and Housing Price and Immigration.
